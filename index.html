About

Built by Martin Kagan.
35 Years In The Making.

Martin Kagan spent over three decades in numerical calibration and multi-dimensional data analysis before asking a simple question: why can't I see all of this at once?

In 2010 he began designing B4_UR_IZ. In 2013 he founded Data Cocoon LLC. By 2016, the system was patented. Today it stands as a unique Cartesian plane capable of visually projecting data in and over time — in 3D — in a way that enhances cognitive recognition through proportional, relative visual patterns.

Pie charts show a moment. Bar graphs show a comparison. B4_UR_IZ tells the whole story.
